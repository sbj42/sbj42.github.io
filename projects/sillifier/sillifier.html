Words are classified by likely part-of-speech and then randomly transformed into similar words. Based on data from the Moby project. Sameness adjusts how many transformable words are transformed. Simplity adjusts the likelihood that a transformed word will come from the "1,000 most frequently used English words" versus the "first edition of the Official Scrabble Players Dictionary(tm)".
